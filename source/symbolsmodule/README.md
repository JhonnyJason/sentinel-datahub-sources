# Module for handling Symbols
Here we carry the informtion of what symbols are relevant for us. 

## Relevant Symbols
We maintain a list of relevant Symbols and their respective raw sources in  `./symbols-collection/`.

We look at limited collections according to following wish-list:

- Currencies
- Commodities without Livestock and Agricultural
- Symbols in Nasdaq Composite
- Symbols in Dow Jones
- Symbols in Russel 2000
- Symbols in EuroStoxx 50
- Symbols in DAX 40
- 


## Available Files:
- Currencies: `./symbols-collection/currencies.json`
- All Commodities on MarketStack: `./symbols-collection/marketstack-commodities.xlsx`
- S&P 500 Constituents: `./symbols-collection/sp500.json`
- Dow Jones Constituents: `./symbols-collection/dow-jones.json`
